Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my travel documents?

To add your travel documents:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap on "Travel Documents"
  3. Select "Add Document"
  4. Choose between Passport or Visa
  5. Fill in the required information

How do notifications work?

GoTravelApp sends notifications for:

  • Document expiry reminders (90 and 30 days before)
  • Trip preparation reminders (7 and 1 day before)
  • Important travel updates

You can customize notification settings in the Profile tab.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Your data is only accessible to you

How do I update the app?

To update GoTravelApp:

  1. Open the App Store on your device
  2. Tap on your profile picture
  3. Scroll to find GoTravelApp
  4. Tap "Update" if available
